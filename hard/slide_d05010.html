JSP Lab

If you've been using Java for some time, the material in the lecture topics was a review. For experienced developers, we've provided a lab for you to try on your own.
  • To begin the lab, download labsetup_v2.doc.
  • Open labsetup_v2.doc; read and follow the directions.
  • To complete the last step in labsetup, you'll need jsptutoriallabfiles.zip.
  • Finally, download jsp_lab.doc.
  • Open jsp_lab.doc and work through the lab exercise.
